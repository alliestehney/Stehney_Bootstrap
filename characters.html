Rachel Green

Rachel Green is played by Jennifer Aniston; she is a fashion guru who starts out as a waitress when she first moves to New York City, but ends up working for Ralph Lauren. She has a daughter, Emma, with Ross later on in the show.

Chandler Bing

Chandler Bing is known as the 'funny guy' on the show and ends up marrying Monica Geller. He makes a career change and moves into advertising, which lends to funny episodes as him working as an unpaid intern.

Phoebe Buffet

Phoebe Buffet seems like the wild card character throughout most of the seasons. She loves playing guitar and earns money by giving massages. She marries a man named Mike - not featured on this characters page - towards the end of the show. Fun Fact (which I'm sure you mega-fans already know!): she has a twin!

Joey Tribiani

Joey is an actor and plays a Days of our Lives. When he's not eating all of the food out of Chandler and Monica's fridge, he's taking a new girl on a date across town. He's a hilarious character who adds a lot to the show! Don't you agree?.

Ross Geller

Ross is known for his love for dinosaurs -- and Rachel. Him and Rachel have an on-again off-again relationship and end up having a daughter, Emma. A famous line from the show - and one of my favorites - is "he's her lobster." This line comes to fruition when the two decide to be together in the season 10 finale. He's an awkward, but hilarious, character who adds a lot to the show.

Monica Geller

Monica is a hardworking chef who dates Richard - the man with the mustache - for a good portion of the show. However, she ends up with Chandler after they decide to start dating after the group's trip to London. She's very organized and loves to help clean!
